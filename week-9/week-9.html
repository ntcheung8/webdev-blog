Description

This week I learned about adding google maps to a website on developers.google.com. Google maps seems to be used quite often in restaurant and business sites. Placing a marker on the map ended up being a lot simpler than I thought it would be, so I checked out some of the other tutorials provided by Google and I found a neat real-time collaborative marking map here. Although real-time mapping tutorial was a little more complicated, it was interesting to learn a little bit about firebase. The most complicated part of the below sample was probably getting the APIKEY working properly from Google's site. I had to setup credentials, billing, and pick the correct api, in this case it was "Maps JavaScript API."
